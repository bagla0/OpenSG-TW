# Citing OpenSG-TW

OpenSG-TW implements published Mechanics-of-Structure-Genome and VABS theory; if you use it, please cite the
underlying methods (full list on the {doc}`references` page), in particular:

> W. Yu, D. H. Hodges, J. C. Ho. *Variational asymptotic beam sectional analysis — an updated version.*
> International Journal of Engineering Science **59** (2012) 40–64.

and the OpenSG project it extends:

> OpenSG — W. Yu group, Purdue University. <https://github.com/wenbinyugroup/OpenSG>

The Reissner–Mindlin transverse-shear treatment additionally follows the MITC / assumed-natural-strain
literature (Dvorkin & Bathe 1984–1986; Barlow 1976; Prathap & Bhashyam 1982) — see {doc}`references`.
